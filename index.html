WELCOME TO MOUNTAIN INN

TREK SUPPORT

ADVENTURE AWAITS

Explore the Himalayan Trails

Dharamshala and the surrounding areas offer some of the most breathtaking trekking opportunities in the Himalayas. From beginner-friendly trails to challenging multi-day expeditions, there's something for every adventure enthusiast.

At Mountain Inn, we provide comprehensive trek support services to make your adventure safe and memorable:

  • Expert local guides familiar with all regional trails
  • Trekking equipment rental (hiking poles, backpacks, etc.)
  • Packed lunches and snacks for day treks
  • Transportation to and from trailheads
  • Custom itinerary planning for multi-day treks
  • Emergency support and first aid

Popular Treks from Dharamshala:

Triund (Easy, 1 day) Indrahar Pass (Moderate, 3 days) Kareri Lake (Moderate, 2 days) Minkiani Pass (Difficult, 4 days)
